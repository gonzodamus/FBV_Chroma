First time here? Build the patched firmware ›

Turn your own copy of the stock Line 6 firmware into FBV Chroma 1.2, right here in your browser. Nothing is uploaded; the patch happens on your device.

  1. Get Line 6's stock firmware file, Fbv3_v1_02_00.hxf (from the Line 6 FBV3 Updater, or Line 6's site).
  2. Choose it below. Your patched Fbv3_Chroma_1.2.hxf downloads automatically.
  3. Flash it with the Line 6 FBV3 Updater (update from a file).

Apply to all:

Save LED settings to pedal writes the current colors and behaviors to the pedal's memory so they survive a power-cycle. The pedal will restart after saving.

Sends Control Change on channel 1: CC# = LED index, value = state × 8 + color. Behavior: CC 16, value = ledIndex × 4 + behavior. Save LEDs to memory: CC 17 = 1. MIDI assignments use the pedal's config SysEx (F0 00 01 0C 11 03 … F7). Requires the patched firmware (FBV Chroma 1.2) and a Chromium browser served over https or localhost.
